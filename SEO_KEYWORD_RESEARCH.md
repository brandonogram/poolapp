# SEO Keyword Research for PoolApp

**Last Updated:** 2026-02-01
**Target Domain:** poolapp-tau.vercel.app

---

## Executive Summary

This keyword research identifies 30+ high-value keywords for PoolApp to target. The focus is on keywords that pool service business owners and operators would search when looking for routing software, business management tools, and efficiency solutions.

**Key Findings:**
- "Pool service software" keywords have moderate-high search volume with medium competition
- Long-tail keywords around specific pain points (route optimization, callbacks, chemistry tracking) have lower competition and higher conversion intent
- Comparison keywords (e.g., "Skimmer vs Jobber") represent high-intent bottom-of-funnel traffic
- Local SEO opportunities exist for targeting geographic markets

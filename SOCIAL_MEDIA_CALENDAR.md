## Cross-Posting Strategy

### Platform Differences

| Aspect | Twitter/X | LinkedIn |
|--------|-----------|----------|
| Tone | Casual, direct, punchy | Professional, insightful |
| Length | Short (under 280 chars ideal) | Long-form OK (1,300 chars sweet spot) |
| Hashtags | 2-3 per post | 3-5 per post |
| Posting Frequency | 2-3x daily | 3-4x weekly |
| Best Days | Every day | Mon, Tue, Wed, Thu |
| Best Times | 7-9 AM, 12-1 PM, 6-8 PM ET | 9-11 AM ET |
| Content Style | Tips, stats, testimonials | Stories, insights, case studies |

### Adaptation Guidelines

**Twitter -> LinkedIn:**
1. Expand the tweet into a full story
2. Add context and background
3. Include a question to drive engagement
4. Make tone more professional
5. Add 2-3 more hashtags

**LinkedIn -> Twitter:**
1. Extract the key stat or insight
2. Cut to the most compelling point
3. Make it punchy and direct
4. Reduce to 2-3 hashtags
5. Add urgency or hook

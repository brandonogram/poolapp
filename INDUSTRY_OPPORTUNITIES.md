## 10. Competitor Analysis

### Direct Competitors (Pool-Specific Software)

| Competitor | Pricing | Users | Strengths | Weakness |
|------------|---------|-------|-----------|----------|
| **[Skimmer](https://www.getskimmer.com/)** | $49+/month | 30,000+ | Market leader, robust features | Expensive at scale |
| **[Pool Brain](https://poolbrain.com/)** | $39+/month | Growing | Heritage partnership, AI features | Less established |
| **[Pool360 PoolService](https://portal.pool360.com/)** | Varies | POOLCORP users | Distributor integration | Tied to POOLCORP |
| **[Paythepoolman](https://www.paythepoolman.com/)** | $49+/month | Growing | Payment focus | Limited features |
| **[Pool Office Manager](https://poolofficemanager.com/)** | $29+/month | Smaller | Affordable | Fewer integrations |

### Horizontal Competitors

| Competitor | Pricing | Focus | Threat Level |
|------------|---------|-------|--------------|
| **[ServiceTitan](https://www.servicetitan.com/)** | $398+/month | All trades | Medium (expensive, not pool-specific) |
| **[Jobber](https://getjobber.com/)** | $39+/month | All trades | High (affordable, good marketing) |
| **[Housecall Pro](https://www.housecallpro.com/)** | $49+/month | All trades | Medium (not pool-specific) |

**Competitive Positioning:**
- Price competitively with Skimmer ($49 or less)
- Emphasize pool-specific features vs. horizontal platforms
- Build unique integrations (equipment manufacturers, distributors)
- Focus on ease of use for solo operators

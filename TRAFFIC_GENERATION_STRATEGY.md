## Executive Summary

PoolApp is positioned to capture the fragmented pool service software market. The industry is dominated by generic routing tools (Route4Me, Badger Maps) that charge premium prices without pool-specific features, and legacy solutions with outdated UIs.

**Our competitive advantages:**
- Pool-specific chemistry tracking (prevents callbacks)
- No per-driver seat fees (vs $15/driver/month for Route4Me)
- $39-79/month vs $150-225/month for competitors
- Simple setup (5 minutes vs 30+ minutes)
- ROI of 500-1,600% in first year

**Traffic strategy focus areas:**
1. **Immediate (0-30 days):** Direct outreach via LinkedIn and email
2. **Short-term (30-90 days):** Content marketing, Twitter presence, partnerships
3. **Long-term (90+ days):** SEO, industry authority building, paid advertising

**Target acquisition cost:** <$50/customer
**Target payback period:** <2 months
